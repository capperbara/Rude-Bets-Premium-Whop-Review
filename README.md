Sports betting underwent major changes following the 2018 Supreme Court decision in Murphy v. National Collegiate Athletic Association.

The Professional and Amateur Sports Protection Act of 1992 (PASPA) previously made sports betting illegal nationwide, with exceptions for Delaware, Montana, Nevada, and Oregon.

* 30 states plus D.C. and Puerto Rico allow online sports betting
